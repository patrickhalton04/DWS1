Recipe For Labarbera Family Sauce

Submitted By: Patrick Halton

Cook Time: 8 hours

Recipe Take From My Mother's Side of the Family

"Sauce made from Italian Ancestors"

Ingrediants:

  • 3/4 cup Garlic
  • 1/2 cup Onion
  • 3 cans of RED PACK Tomatos
  • 3 cans of RED PACK Tomato Paste
  • 1/4 cup of Basil
  • 1/4 cup of Oregano
  • 3 cans of Water

Directions:

  1. Put Stove on Low Heat
  2. Place Sauce Pot On stove
  3. Pour 3 cans of red pack, 3 cans of tomato paste, and 3 cans of water into sauce pot
  4. Let sit for 4 hours, stir occasionally
  5. Put Garlic, Onion, Basil, and Oregano into sauce pot
  6. Let sit for additional 4 hours and stir occasional until ready
